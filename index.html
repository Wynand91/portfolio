Nice to meet you!

My name is Wynand Theron, and I’m a software developer based in Cape Town, South Africa.

After finishing school, I felt the pressure to make a “responsible” choice, so I pursued a Bachelor of Science degree at Stellenbosch University, one of South Africa’s top universities. While I completed the degree, I eventually realized it wasn’t the path I truly wanted to follow — and job prospects in South Africa for that field were limited at the time.

Two years later, I went back to college with a new plan: marketing. While applying for jobs, I noticed one listing that caught my eye — with a specific requirement: “knowledge of HTML/CSS beneficial.” That single requirement changed everything for me. Unable to afford university again due to debt from my first degree, I turned to free online resources like Codecademy and FreeCodeCamp. I dove headfirst into coding, quickly falling in love with the craft and forgetting about my past paths.

Since then, I’ve built over 6 years of experience as a backend developer at a great company called Byte Orbit, specializing in FinTech. I’ve worked extensively with Python and Django, designing and maintaining secure, scalable APIs, building complex systems like payment processing interfaces, and integrating with cloud services such as AWS S3 and SES. Along the way, I’ve grown comfortable with Docker, REST API design, relational databases, and exploring new technologies like Golang and TypeScript.

I’m passionate about writing clean, efficient code that solves real problems, and I’m always excited to keep learning and tackling new challenges.
