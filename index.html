Hi, I’m Wynand Theron — a year old backend developer based in Cape Town, South Africa.

I’ve spent over 6 years building scalable, secure systems in the FinTech space, at a company called Byte Orbit, primarily using Python and Django. I worked on everything from payment gateways to cloud-based integrations (AWS S3, SES), and built REST APIs that power real-world financial tools.

My path into software wasn’t traditional. After a BSc degree and a brief foray into marketing, a job ad sparked my curiosity with one line: “HTML/CSS knowledge beneficial.” That led me to free online courses like Codecademy and FreeCodeCamp — and soon, to a deep love of coding.

I’m passionate about writing clean, efficient code that solves real problems, and I’m always excited to keep learning and tackling new challenges.
